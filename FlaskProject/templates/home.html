What is Fake News?

“Fake news” is a term used to refer to fabricated news. Fake news is an invention – a lie created out of nothing – that takes the appearance of real news with the aim of deceiving people. This is what is important to remember: the information is false, but it seems true. That’s logical! If it is too obvious that it is a lie, it won’t have any impact. Fake news is a little like a false rumour, but on a large scale… Just as true information has many faces, fake news can also come in many forms. Here are a few of them.
